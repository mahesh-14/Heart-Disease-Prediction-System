Possible Presence of Heart Disease

The prediction suggests that the person may have heart disease, but further evaluation by a healthcare professional is recommended.

It's important to seek medical attention and follow your healthcare provider's recommendations.

Here are some general tips for managing heart disease:

  • Follow a heart-healthy diet low in saturated fats, trans fats, and cholesterol.
  • Monitor blood pressure, cholesterol levels, and blood sugar regularly.
  • Take prescribed medications as directed by your healthcare provider.
  • Engage in regular physical activity as recommended by your healthcare provider.
  • Quit smoking and avoid exposure to secondhand smoke.
  • Manage stress through relaxation techniques and support networks.
